# Manage Credits

Credits allow you to adjust customer balances for overpayments, refunds, or other billing scenarios. This section explains how to view, issue, and manage credits in iBill.io.

---

## Credits List Overview
- **ID**: Unique credit identifier
- **Invoice ID**: Linked invoice (if applicable)
- **Customer**: The customer receiving the credit
- **Reference**: Description or reason for the credit
- **Date of Credit**
- **Amount Paid**
- **Reversed**: Indicates if the credit has been reversed
- **Actions**: Options to manage the credit

   ![Credits List](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6037e42f8502d1120e90b430/file-i8d5jiG37S.png)

## Credit Actions
- **Reverse Credit**: Undo a credit if issued in error
- **Email Credit Receipt**: Send a receipt to the customer
- **Download Credit Receipt**: Save a PDF copy for your records

   ![Credit Actions](htt1ps://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6037e3410a2dae5b58fb37e3/file-6P8utOEXas.png)

## Best Practices
- Issue credits promptly for overpayments or adjustments
- Keep detailed references for each credit
- Regularly review credits for accuracy
