# Adding A New Reseller

<h2></h2><h2><u>Adding a New Reseller:</u></h2><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/609427f9cb6bca73071dc50b/file-FOOjR8QhwH.png" style="width: 1313px;">To create a new reseller, you will need to fill out the following information:&nbsp;</p><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/609144543149d33a19c73538/file-YatE4ta62m.jpg"></p><ul>
	
<li>The name of the reseller.&nbsp;</li>	
<li>The email address of the reseller.</li>	
<li>The type of commission that the reseller will be getting.&nbsp;</li>	
<li>The password for this reseller.&nbsp;</li></ul><p>
	The following is options are available with the 'Commission Type' dropdown</p><ul>
	
<li>No commission</li>	
<li>Percentage per invoice paid (after customer has paid)</li>	
<li>Fixed amount per invoice paid (after the customer has paid)</li>	
<li>Percentage of sale (before the customer pays)</li>	
<li>Fixed amount per sale (before the customer pays)&nbsp;</li></ul><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/609428f94d12c85c420cc529/file-HrIgrTwR0t.png" style="width: 799px;">You also have the option to fill in additional information including:&nbsp;</p><ul>
	
<li><span style="font-weight: normal;">Reseller contact information</span></li>	
<li><span style="font-weight: normal;">The name of the company.</span></li>	
<li><span style="font-weight: normal;">The company tax number.</span></li>	
<li><span style="font-weight: normal;">The company registration number</span></li></ul><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6091455e8996210f18bd9fde/file-rhwR3Qmv7W.jpg"></p><p>
	 In addition to this, you have the ability to capture the resellers address details as seen below:&nbsp;</p><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/609145bb8af76a714bfddcb0/file-oZOE3zI9XH.jpg"></p><p>
	 Alternatively, you can manually enter the address details of the reseller with the below interface:&nbsp;</p><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/60914604f8c0ef2d98df90b6/file-Gd2JwR8EZK.jpg"></p><p>
	 Once you have filled in all the relevant fields, you can create a new reseller by clicking the "Create Reseller" button:&nbsp;</p><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/609148a63149d33a19c73551/file-wJbIgnMsv6.jpg">This will take you to a new page where you can view the recorded information of the reseller, which you created:&nbsp;</p><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/609149343149d33a19c73556/file-iq6mhdxH60.jpg">This includes:&nbsp;</p><ul>
	
<li>The date, which the reseller account was originally registered.&nbsp;</li>	
<li>Payment information of the reseller.&nbsp;</li></ul><p>
	 If you would like to create notes highlighting specific details for certain resellers, you can do so with the below field. Simply click on the " + New Note" button.&nbsp;</p><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/60914a24f8c0ef2d98df90d0/file-8BPezSEoTm.jpg"></p><p>
	 The below interface will pop up, enter the notes, which you would like to highlight for this particular reseller, and then click the "Add Note" button.&nbsp;</p><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/60914a9af8c0ef2d98df90d3/file-if0UAMZwbm.jpg"></p><p>
	 Once you have created a note, you will be able to see the note in the section as well as:&nbsp;</p><ul>
	
<li>The date on which the note was created.</li>	
<li>A brief of the note itself.</li>	
<li>Who created the note.</li>	
<li>You are also able to edit or delete the note with the two action icons.</li></ul><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/60914ec1f8c0ef2d98df9107/file-6RsV4RtYpQ.jpg"></p><p>
	 You can also handle payments for the reseller through the below section, in order to do so, click on the button "+ New Payment"&nbsp;</p><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/60914b838af76a714bfddcd1/file-bk4SaFx1A4.jpg" style="width: 842px;"></p><p>
	 This will take you to a new page where you can see all the information relating to invoices for that particular reseller.&nbsp;</p><p>
	You will be able to view paid invoices as well as any outstanding invoices that need to be paid to that reseller.</p><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/60914bdc3149d33a19c7356d/file-mQ0rcEnTTm.jpg"></p><p>
	 You are also able to update all your reseller's information in this section.</p><p>
	<em style="background-color: initial;">Please click on our "Support" button at the top right of the page if you have not found any information to assist with what you needed in this section.</em></p><p>
