# Hotspots Setup

Hotspots allow you to provide flexible, voucher-based internet access at specific locations. This section explains how to enable, configure, and manage your hotspots in iBill.io.

---

## Setting Up Your Hotspots
1. **Enable Hotspots**: Use the checkbox to enable hotspot functionality. Click **Update Settings** to apply changes.
   ![Enable Hotspots](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6037ed6a0a2dae5b58fb3839/file-adAgQlFB4Z.png)
2. **Use Hotspot Vouchers**: Once enabled, users can log in with hotspot vouchers. If disabled, only regular data services can log in to your hotspot controllers.
3. **Disable Hotspots**: Click the green tick to disable hotspots, then update settings.
   ![Disable Hotspots](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6037edb08502d1120e90b486/file-HMZXiJntVg.png)

---

Hotspots are ideal for temporary or public access scenarios, such as cafes, hotels, or events. For more advanced configuration, refer to the Hotspot Vouchers section.
