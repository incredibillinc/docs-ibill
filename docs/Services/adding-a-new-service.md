# Adding a New Service

<p>
	<u style="background-color: initial; font-family: inherit; font-size: 21px; font-weight: 700;">Adding a New Service:</u></p><p>
	To add a new service, firstly select the drop-down box.&nbsp; (You will be able to search for customer names as well).&nbsp;</p><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6036bb4224d2d21e45ed9e50/file-9mwUSD2kK2.png">Once you have selected your customer name, you will automatically go to the following page:</p><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6036bbabb3ebfb109b585d02/file-Yfaka8zKQX.png">Below the customer's information, you are able to use the drop-down box, to select your product you would like to assign to the customer once you have selected the product - the product information will be available to edit:</p><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6036bc1f0a2dae5b58fb3322/file-usGX3EPQ6q.png">The following product information can be edited within this section</p><ul>
	
<li>You are able to add the description</li>	
<li>Username - Service username, (a realm with be automatically appended).</li>	
<li>Service Password, if left blank - a password will be generated, you can then view the service to see the password.</li></ul><p>
	You are also able to edit the Billing information for the product:</p><ul>
	
<li>You are able to adjust the price</li></ul><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6036be9624d2d21e45ed9e71/file-dQJyOCPJiH.png" style="width: 467px;"></p><ul>
	
<li>Able to view the Billing cycle</li>	
<li>Able to edit the next invoice date</li></ul><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6036bee38502d1120e90af3a/file-6AvwyCuIgi.png" style="width: 388px;"></p><p>
	Once you have completed your Product and Billing information, click the "Create service" to complete this section:</p><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6036bfa124d2d21e45ed9e7c/file-Q3VmnQVXkR.png"></p><p>
	You will then be directed to the service, where you are able to view and edit the service.</p><p>
	The following service information will be available:</p><ul>
	
<li>Service Info</li>	
<li>Customer</li>	
<li>Product</li>	
<li>Status</li>	
<li>Creation date</li>	
<li>Last modified</li>	
<li>Service Type</li></ul><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6036c0be661b720174a70a63/file-pdaCtUlHqE.png" style="width: 414px;"></p><p>
	The following billing information will be available to be edited:</p><ul>
	
<li>Service price</li>	
<li>Discount</li>	
<li>Final price</li>	
<li>Billing cycle</li>	
<li>Billing date&nbsp;</li>	
<li>Billing status</li>	
<li>Last invoice</li>	
<li>Next invoice</li></ul><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6036c0e58502d1120e90af4a/file-LChztey9IV.png" style="width: 428px;"></p><p>
	The next section allows you to see the following information:</p><ul>
	
<li>General info (Radius username/password, Static IP, Active Sessions)</li>	
<li>Radius AVP Pairs - Attributes&nbsp;can be added.&nbsp; Radius attributes specified here will be sent whenever this service connects)</li>	
<li>Cap information</li>	
<li>Usage&nbsp;(Can be set to 24 Hours, Last week, or last month)</li>	
<li>Graphs (Can be set to 24 Hours, Last week, or last month)</li>	
<li>Auth logs</li>	
<li>Sessions</li>	
<li>Port locking</li>	
<li>Location</li></ul><p>
	<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6036c1f624d2d21e45ed9e8e/file-tfMcoE5k9y.png">Below this, you are able to add tags, service notes, and add equipment assigned to the service.<img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6036c2268502d1120e90af5a/file-x86jOtebkX.png"></p><blockquote>
	<em>Please click on our "Support" button at the top right of the page if you have not found any information to assist with what you needed in this section.</em>
</blockquote>
