# Manage Customers

Managing your customers is central to your business operations. This section allows you to view, edit, communicate with, and organize your customers efficiently within iBill.io.

---

## Customer List Overview
- **Customer ID**
- **Name**
- **Phone Number**
- **Email Address**
- **Date Joined**
- **Balance**
- **Services**
- **Tags**
- **Status**

   ![Customer List](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6036afcb24d2d21e45ed9dea/file-W5LVfTYGZw.png)

## Filtering and Bulk Actions
- Use the filter box in the top right to quickly find customers.
- Select multiple customers to enable, disable, delete, edit, or send emails/texts in bulk.

   ![Filter Box](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6036a74ab3ebfb109b585c2e/file-FXW8gZKHme.png)

## Editing Customer Details
- Click the edit icon next to a customer to view and update their full information, including:
  - Registered date
  - Services
  - Account balance
  - Invoices
  - Notes, contacts, tags, FX rates, credits, and more
  - Assign to a reseller
  - Billing settings (including suspension options)
  - Location

   ![Edit Customer](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6036b5f8661b720174a709e7/file-p717ihTTYK.png)

## Exporting and Adding Customers
- Export your customer list as a CSV file.
- Add new customers directly from this section.
