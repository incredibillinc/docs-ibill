# Creating a Generic Product

Generic Products are flexible, one-time items or services you can offer to customers (e.g., hardware, setup fees, or non-recurring services). This guide explains how to create a new Generic Product in iBill.io.

---

## Step-by-Step: Creating a Generic Product

1. **Select Product Type**
   - Choose whether this is a service or a product. For most generic products, select 'Product' (one-time cost).

   ![Product Type](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/5b4c4fc40428631d7a88ed9c/img-2564-1531727769-1183672208.png)

2. **Enter Product Information**
   - Name and describe the product.

   ![Product Info](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/5b4c4fc40428631d7a88ed9d/img-2564-1531727769-921663805.png)

3. **Set Pricing**
   - Enter the one-time price (taxable if applicable).

   ![Pricing](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/5b4c4fc42c7d3a03f89c97c3/img-2564-1531727769-718492990.png)

4. **Review and Confirm**
   - Check all details and click Create Product to finalize.

   ![Confirm Product](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/5b4c4fc40428631d7a88ed9e/img-2564-1531727769-451595256.png)
