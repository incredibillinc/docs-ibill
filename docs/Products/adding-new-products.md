# Adding New Products

Adding new products in iBill.io allows you to offer a wide range of services and add-ons to your customers. Products can be regular (core services) or bolt-on (add-ons and upgrades). This guide explains the types of products you can create and how to use the Product Creation Wizard.

---

## Product Types
- **Regular Products and Services**: Core offerings such as Data Service, VOIP Service, and Generic Products.
- **Bolt-on Products and Services**: Add-ons like Data Topup, Data Booster, VoIP Bundles, Static IP, and Static Netblock Products.

   ![Product Types](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/604125c7b3ebfb109b5889eb/file-BF50RRVlPZ.png)

## How to Add a New Product
1. **Select Product Type**: Choose between Regular or Bolt-on products.
2. **Follow the Product Creation Wizard**: The wizard will guide you through:
   - Product/service selection
   - Product information (name, description)
   - Pricing and billing options
   - Service-specific settings (e.g., data limits, IP pools)
   - Confirmation and review
3. **Save the Product**: Once complete, your new product will be available for assignment to customers.

## Best Practices
- Use clear, descriptive names for products.
- Regularly review your product catalog to ensure offerings are up to date.
- Use bolt-on products to provide flexible upgrades and add-ons for customers.
