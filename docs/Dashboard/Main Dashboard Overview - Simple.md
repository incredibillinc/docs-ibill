## Dashboard Metrics

- **Services Online**: Number of users currently connected and the percentage of total users online.

- **Authentication Failures**: Number and percentage of failed login attempts in the last 60 minutes.

- **New Sessions**: Number of sessions started in the last hour compared to the previous hour.

- **NAS Online**: Number and percentage of NAS devices currently online. NAS devices are managed under *RADIUS > Manage NAS Devices*.

## Recent Activity

Shows the most recent invoices. Each invoice includes:

- Customer name

- Invoice date and due date

- Payment status

- Provisioning status

Quick links let you open the invoice or the customer details page directly.

---

## Upcoming Suspensions

Shows accounts that may be suspended because of unpaid invoices. Each record includes:

- Customer name

- Service ID

- Invoice information

- Days overdue

- Planned suspension date

This helps you take action early and keep customers.

---

## Events

- **System Events**: Changes to user accounts, system settings, and admin actions.

- **RADIUS Events**: Alerts for sessions, failed logins, disconnects, and other events.

## Data Usage

The **Data Usage** graph shows upload and download data. You can view:

- Daily trends

- Monthly trends

- Yearly trends

This helps you understand customer traffic patterns.
