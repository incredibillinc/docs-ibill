# General Settings

## Overview
The General Settings section contains organization-wide configuration options for your iBill.io system. Proper setup ensures your business runs smoothly and securely.

---

## What You Can Configure
- Company name, address, and contact details
- Default language and time zone
- Branding (logo, colors)
- System-wide notification preferences
- Tax and compliance settings

---

## Step-by-Step: Updating General Settings
1. Navigate to **Setup > General Settings** in the main menu.
2. Review your current organization details and settings.
3. Click **Edit** to update any field.
4. Save your changes.

> **Best Practice:** Review general settings after major business changes (e.g., address, branding, tax rates).

---

## Troubleshooting
- If you cannot update a setting, check your permissions or contact your administrator.
- For branding issues, ensure logo files meet the required format and size.
