# Payments (Setup)

## Overview
The Payments section in Setup allows you to configure payment gateways, accepted payment methods, and related settings for your iBill.io system. Proper configuration ensures smooth, secure, and reliable payment processing for your customers and resellers.

---

## What You Can Configure
- Add or edit payment gateways (e.g., credit card, PayPal, bank transfer)
- Set default payment methods
- Configure payment notification settings
- Enable or disable payment options for customers and resellers

---

## Step-by-Step: Configuring Payments
1. Navigate to **Setup > Payments** in the main menu.
2. Review the list of available payment gateways and methods.
3. Click **Add Gateway** or **Edit** to configure a payment method.
4. Enter required credentials and settings (API keys, account numbers, etc.).
5. Save your changes.

> **Best Practice:** Test each payment gateway after setup to ensure successful transactions.

---

## Troubleshooting
- For failed payments, check gateway credentials and network connectivity.
- If a payment method is missing, verify your account permissions and gateway availability.
