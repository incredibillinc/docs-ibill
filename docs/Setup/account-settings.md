# Account Settings

## Overview
The Account Settings section allows you to manage your personal and organizational account details in iBill.io. Keeping your account information up to date ensures secure access and smooth communication.

---

## What You Can Do
- Update your email address, phone number, and contact details.
- Change your account password.
- Set notification preferences (e.g., email alerts, SMS notifications).
- Manage two-factor authentication (2FA) for enhanced security.

---

## Step-by-Step: Updating Account Settings
1. Navigate to **Setup > Account Settings** in the main menu.
2. Review your current account details.
3. Click **Edit** to update any field.
4. Save your changes.

> **Best Practice:** Use a strong, unique password and enable 2FA for maximum security.

---

## Troubleshooting
- If you cannot update your details, check your permissions or contact your administrator.
- For password reset issues, use the "Forgot Password" link or contact support.
