# Managing Invoices

Managing invoices is a key part of your billing workflow in iBill.io. This section explains how to view, edit, and process invoices for your customers.

---

## Invoice List Overview
- **ID**: Unique invoice identifier
- **Customer Number**
- **Customer Name**
- **Created Date**
- **Due Date**
- **Total**
- **Total Paid**
- **Paid Status**
- **Provisioned Status**

   ![Invoice List](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6037b8bf0a2dae5b58fb369f/file-Xkne2J16J8.png)

## Invoice Actions
- **Edit, Disable, Delete, Enable, Provision, Mark as Paid**: Use the icons to the right of each invoice for quick actions.
- **Bulk Actions**: Select multiple invoices using the checkboxes to edit, enable, disable, or delete in bulk.

   ![Bulk Actions](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6037b977b3ebfb109b586078/file-usoodbac5b.png)

## Viewing and Editing Invoices
- **Credit Items**
- **Reverse Payments, Email, or Download Invoice Payments**
- **Add Credits**
- **Add Notes**
