# Create New Invoice

Creating new invoices in iBill.io is a streamlined process that ensures accurate billing and record-keeping. Invoices are generated from quotes, allowing you to review and confirm all details before billing your customer.

---

## How to Create a New Invoice

1. **Create a Quote**: Select the customer you want to bill and create a quote for their services or products.
   ![Select Customer](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/60379f0b8502d1120e90b227/file-aoZsba1WXp.png)

2. **Review Billing Information**: Check the quote details, including who created it, customer info, and contact details.
   ![Billing Info](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/60379f5cb3ebfb109b585ff1/file-UFbzNrWO9J.png)

3. **Add Quote Items**: Use the dropdown to add products or services, then edit descriptions, prices, discounts, and service settings as needed.
   ![Add Item](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6037a9f78502d1120e90b26a/file-1P7neMQRnM.png)

4. **Review and Finalize Quote**: Confirm all items, add notes, and review the quote summary.
   ![Quote Summary](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6037ad68b3ebfb109b58603d/file-2kjQjAVneR.png)

5. **Send or Download Quote**: Options include sending the quote, downloading as PDF, or proceeding to create an invoice.
   ![Send Quote](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5b4c4e430428631d7a88ed10/images/6037ae130a2dae5b58fb3668/file-LMnpy2lZQw.png)

6. **Create Invoice**: Click **Create Invoice** to generate the invoice from the quote. The invoice will be populated with all relevant details for review and processing.
